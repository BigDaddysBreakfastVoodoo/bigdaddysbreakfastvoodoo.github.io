Track 01 – Intro (0:28)

Behind the Music

No words, just the swamp breathing. Crickets, frogs, birds, and the night alive. Bart insisted, Mike and Peter reluctantly followed, letting nature set the stage. A soundscape to tie the songs together, as restless as the swamp itself.

Track 02 – Huggin’ tha Bowl (3:18)

Behind the Music

Huggin’ tha Bowl — forged during a Hertog Jan night deep in the southern Netherlands, where blacking out became an obligation. Bart came alive, Mike rolled out the heavy cannons too early, and the evening dissolved fast. Morning found Mike clutching a bucket, hissing like a broken espresso machine brewing stomach acid. Laughter, suffering, and survival — huggin’ tha bowl.

Lyrics

Track 03 – Go the Fuck to Sleep (5:12)

Behind the Music

Go the Fuck to Sleep — a lullaby forged in exhaustion and poor planning. Borrowing its name from Adam Mansbach’s bedtime book, this song is a plea, a curse, and a truth every sleepless soul knows.

Lyrics

Track 04 – You Dawg (4:09)

Behind the Music

Bart strikes again — late night, too much booze, reading the room all wrong. Chaos and mischief wrapped in one wild howl. That’s our drummer.
