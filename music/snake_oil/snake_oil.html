Track 01 – Huggin' tha Bowl (3:21)

Behind the Music

Huggin’ tha Bowl — forged during a Hertog Jan night deep in the southern Netherlands, where blacking out became an obligation. Bart came alive, Mike rolled out the heavy cannons too early, and the evening dissolved fast. Morning found Mike clutching a bucket, hissing like a broken espresso machine brewing stomach acid. This wasn’t the only time when Mike's body created these ominous sounds. On a Belgian tour, Peter and Bart rose hung-over but cheerful, while Mike whimpered in the camper, hugging the sheets as the road rolled on. Laughter, suffering, and survival — huggin’ tha bowl.

Track 02 – Shouldn't Have Said Yes (4:56)

Behind the Music

Shouldn’t Have Said Yes — a hymn to bad decisions and the wisdom that arrives too late. It began with a friend, grey-faced at the bar, gripping his beer like a lifeline after a doctor’s visit best left undescribed. Larger than life, suddenly quiet, recounting probing hands and vanished dignity. Captain Hindsight took the wheel. Maybe lived, maybe learned, maybe wisely denied. A good boy never tells — but the song does.

Track 03 – Go the Fuck to Sleep (4:42)

Behind the Music

Go the Fuck to Sleep — a lullaby forged in exhaustion and poor planning. Bart drifted through life just fine until nature answered a casual “let's see what happens” with a toddler and a surprise set of twins. Nights dissolved into feeding rounds, half-read stories, and prayers whispered at closed doors. Love remained, sleep vanished, sanity followed. Borrowing its name from Adam Mansbach’s bedtime book, this song is a plea, a curse, and a truth every sleepless soul knows.
